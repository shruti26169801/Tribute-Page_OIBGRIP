Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Verily, Islam and Hinduism are terms of contrast.
They are used by the true
Divine painter for blending the colours and filling in the outlines.
If it is a mosque,
the call to prayer is chanted in remembrance of Him.
If it is a temple, the balls are rung in yearning for Him alone.

19 February 1630 - 3 April 1680
